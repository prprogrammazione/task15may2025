TYPES OF SWEETENERS FOR GELATO PRODUCTION

Sucrose
The most common sweetener in traditional ice cream. Comes from sugar beet or sugar cane.
Glucose
Improves ice cream texture and reduces ice crystal formation.
Dextrose
Sweeter than glucose but less sweet than sucrose. Also lowers the freezing point.
Fructose
Natural sweetener found in fruits. Sweeter than sucrose.
